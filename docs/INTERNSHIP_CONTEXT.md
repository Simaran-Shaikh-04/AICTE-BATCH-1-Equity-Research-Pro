## Role & Contribution

As a BCom student specializing in Financial Accounting, my primary focus was designing the **financial logic and analytical rules** of the platform:
1.  **Sector-Specific Scoring Benchmarks:** Defining which ratios matter for each of the 12 industry sectors and setting the thresholds (Poor, Average, Good, Excellent) and weightage for composite score grading.
2.  **Forensic Accounting Rules:** Constructing flags for audits, related-party transactions (RPTs), promoter pledging, and contingent liabilities.
3.  **Validation & Testing:** Compiling and testing actual annual reports of NSE-listed companies (e.g., HDFC Bank, ICICI Bank) to verify that Gemini's extraction matches trusted financial portals like Screener.in.

The web application frontend (React/TypeScript) and backend APIs (Express/Node.js) were compiled and generated with AI assistance (Claude Code and Antigravity) to bridge the gap between financial theory and software implementation.

### 1. Indian Corporate Accounting & Disclosures
*   Learned to navigate 300+ page Indian annual reports to locate Consolidated Balance Sheets, P&L accounts, and specific schedules.
*   Studied the structure of **Related Party Disclosures** and **Contingent Liabilities & Commitments** notes to understand off-balance-sheet risks.

*   Understood why traditional EBITDA/Debt-to-Equity models fail for banking institutions.
*   Gained exposure to key RBI regulatory metrics: **Net Interest Margin (NIM)**, **Capital Adequacy Ratio (CAR)**, and asset quality indicators like **Gross NPA %** and **Net NPA %**.

*   Discovered how to use LLMs (Gemini 2.5 Flash) for structured JSON data extraction from PDF documents.
*   Faced and resolved challenges related to AI extraction, such as unit scale errors (converting millions/lakhs/thousands to crores) and year mislabeling.

### 4. Software Development Workflow
*   Gained basic exposure to React state management, Vite bundlers, Express API routes, and Docker deployment.
